Fried Egg Recipe

Description

It's a fried egg. Can't get much simpler than this. If you can't cook this you should probably stick to fast food from now on, just saying.

Ingredients

  • Egg

Steps

  1. Place pan over medium heat.
  2. Once pan has heated, crack open the egg and allow its contents to fall onto the pan.
    • (OPTIONAL): For a crunchier experience, consider also adding the eggshell to the pan.
  3. Once the egg appears to be solid on the bottom, flip the egg with a spatula.
  4. Give the egg like 15 seconds, then remove the egg from the heat.
  5. Consume Egg.
